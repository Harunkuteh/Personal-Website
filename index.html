Portfolio

Elementary School Welcome Camp

In February 2023, I set up a camp to welcome elementary school students to join the camp at Paramit International Institute. My camp is about “Akrat Day Journey”. It will be a walk that requires you to close your eyes and walk into the prepared area. Do not let it be dark. You will encounter something scary and dangerous. It will make the children understand that even if we close our eyes and walk in this world, it is already scary. How scary will it be on the next Akrat Day?
